Best series to watch in 2025

Below are the top 2 series to watch in 2025.
Here you go....

Black Warrant

Vikramaditya Motwane's adaptation of the novel ‘Black Warrant’ has emerged as one of the most compelling Hindi web series of 2025. Based on a jailer's firsthand account of Tihar Jail's notorious operations during the 1980s, the series explores the complex power dynamics and hierarchical structures shaped by caste and religion.

Paatal Lok Season 2

The highly anticipated second season of ‘Paatal Lok’ has returned with a complex narrative centred around peace negotiations between Naga separatist leaders and Indian government officials, mediated by an influential businessman. This groundbreaking storyline marks one of the first prominent representations of Nagaland and its people in Indian cinema.

Panchayat Season 4

One of the best series to watch based on village politics

Mirzapur Season 3

The comeback of Guddu along with Kaleen Bhaiya brings exciting new action and suspenseful storytelling and dramatic elements. Mirzapur Season 3 delivers its audience an explosive and deadly crime series filled with power struggles that must be seen by anyone who admires intense action-packed thrillers.

The Family Man Season 3

Manoj Bajpayee reprises Srikant Tiwari’s role in The Family Man Season 3 while facing elevated risks throughout the story. Expect intensive suspense and significant action moments during the high-risk mission that takes him to China.
